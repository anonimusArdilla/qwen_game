# Economy

## Currency
- Soft currency: Coins
- Coins are earned through play, daily login, and optional rewarded ads

## Earn Rates (Target)
- Stage clear: 30–60 coins depending on stage index
- Perfect micro-stage bonus: +5 coins
- Daily login: 50 / 75 / 100 / 125 / 150 / 175 / 250 (7-day loop)
- Rewarded “double coins” (optional): multiplies the run’s earned coins by 2, capped once per stage

## Sinks (Target)
- Skins: 300–1200 coins (rarity tiers)
- Themes: 800–2000 coins
- Cosmetic preview is free; equip is validated against ownership

## Inflation Controls
- Daily rewards are bounded and looped
- Rewarded multipliers are frequency-limited via remote config once enabled
- High-cost cosmetics provide long-term goals without impacting gameplay balance
